Milk Rose

Description

Here you go, an easy make-ahead recipe, with few ingredients ready in a few minutes. we feel it's worth to be a part of every celebration and festive days. Not only on special occasions but at times, you absolutely deserve a sweet treat for all your hard works. Treat yourself with this delicious drink whenever you need it.

Ingredients

  • Milk
  • Roohafza
  • Ice

Direction

  1. Bring 1 cup of water to boil, add 2 cups of loosely packed dried rose petals.
  2. Boil for 1 minute and switch off the stove.
  3. Cover it with a lid and set aside for 6 hours. This resting time allows the water to infuse with the essence of rose.
  4. And mix this rose milk syrup with milk whenever needed. And you pat yourself for making one of the best homemade cool drinks.
